Christopher Nolan, the celebrated director, has revealed his 12 favorite movies that have inspired his work. Some of these films include "Heat," which influenced his film "Batman Begins," and "Speed," which inspired his war epic "Dunkirk." Nolan also cites "2001: A Space Odyssey" as a film that transported him to another world and influenced his film "Interstellar." Other favorites include "12 Angry Men," "Chariots of Fire," "Alien," "Blade Runner," "The Right Stuff," "Metropolis," "Lawrence of Arabia," "Greed," and "Star Wars," which sparked his passion for filmmaking.
