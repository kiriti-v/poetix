Francis Ford Coppola, the director of films such as The Godfather and Apocalypse Now, revealed that his favorite movie he has made is B'Twixt Now and Sunrise. Released in 2011, the film follows the story of a horror writer who encounters a sheriff and gets involved in a murder investigation. Coppola described it as an enchanting and personal film, although it was initially released as a horror film. He also mentioned that he likes Rumble Fish and the new version of the third Godfather film, titled The Godfather, Coda: The Death of Michael Corleone.
