James Cameron, the director of "Avatar 2," has shared a list of his favorite movies. Some of the films he recommends include "Dr. Strangelove," "Wait Until Dark," "The Woman King," "Borat," "Resident Evil," "Alien," "The Wizard of Oz," "2001: A Space Odyssey," "The Godfather," "Close Encounters of the Third Kind," "Jaws," and "Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid." Cameron has praised these films for their storytelling, performances, and technical achievements.
