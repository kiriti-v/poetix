In 1994, Ingmar Bergman, the Swedish director known for films like Wild Strawberries and Persona, named his 11 favorite films. He praised Andrei Tarkovsky as the greatest director, saying that Tarkovsky moves with naturalness in the room of dreams. Bergman also selected films by directors such as Charlie Chaplin, Federico Fellini, and Akira Kurosawa. The list includes Andrei Rublev, The Circus, The Conductor, Marianne and Juliane, The Passion of Joan of Arc, The Phantom Carriage, Port of Shadows, Raven's End, Rashomon, La strada, and Sunset Boulevard.
