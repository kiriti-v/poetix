Christopher Nolan, the acclaimed director, has shared a list of his favorite movies that have influenced his own work. Some of the titles include "On Her Majesty's Secret Service," "Heat," "The Hateful Eight," "Baby Driver," "Chariots of Fire," "Speed," "2001: A Space Odyssey," "12 Angry Men," "Alien," "All Quiet on the Western Front," "Blade Runner," "Close Encounters of the Third Kind," "First Man," "Foreign Correspondent," and "Greed." These films have inspired Nolan in various ways, from their storytelling techniques to their visual splendor.
