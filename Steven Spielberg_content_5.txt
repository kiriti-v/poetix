Steven Spielberg, one of the most influential film directors in history, shared his top 10 favorite movies of all time. The list includes "Fantasia" (1940), "Citizen Kane" (1941), "A Guy Named Joe" (1943), "It's a Wonderful Life" (1946), "The War of the Worlds" (1953), "Psycho" (1960), "Lawrence of Arabia" (1962), "2001: A Space Odyssey" (1968), "The Godfather" (1972), and "Day for Night" (1973).
