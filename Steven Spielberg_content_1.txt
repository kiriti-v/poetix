Steven Spielberg, the renowned director, has shared a list of his favorite movies. Some of the films he recommends include "Lawrence of Arabia," "Citizen Kane," "Guardians of the Galaxy," "The Godfather," "The Searchers," "Stagecoach," "How Green Was My Valley," "Tootsie," "Captains Courageous," "The Best Years of Our Lives," "Fantasia," "The Shining," and Christopher Nolan's "Dark Knight" trilogy. Spielberg has praised these films for their storytelling, cinematography, and impact on his own work.
